1. Introduction

Welcome to Questify ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Questify mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Questify is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No account registration required
  • No email addresses collected
  • No names or personal identifiers collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No third-party analytics services

3. Data Storage

All data you create in Questify (quests, progress, settings, achievements) is stored locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your data
  • Your data is not backed up to any server
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local data

4. Third-Party Services

Questify does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The app functions entirely offline.

5. Children's Privacy

Questify does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete from our systems. You have full control over your local data on your device.
